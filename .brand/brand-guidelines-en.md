# 🌿 SELVO — Brand Guidelines (English)

---

# 1. 🧭 Brand Overview

## Brand Name

**Selvo**

## Meaning

Selvo represents a fusion of:

- **Secure** → Protection of digital assets
- **Evolution** → Continuous growth in Web3 innovation

It is also inspired by the Latin word **“Silva”** (forest), symbolizing:

- Growth
- Natural ecosystem
- Sustainability

👉 **Selvo stands for a secure and evolving digital asset ecosystem.**

---

# 2. 🧠 Brand Definition

**Selvo is a next-generation Web3 wallet focused on security, simplicity, and continuous evolution in managing digital assets.**

---

# 3. 🎯 Vision & Mission

## Vision

**To become a leading Web3 wallet that empowers users globally to securely grow their digital assets.**

## Mission

- Provide a secure and user-friendly wallet experience
- Simplify access to the Web3 ecosystem
- Continuously innovate with modern blockchain technology
